Best APPs To Record Phone Calls For iPhone

  • by TOPAPP
  • 1356
  • 13
ad test

Call recorder apps have a lot of legitimate uses, and some people like to keep phone calls recorded for legal and security reasons. However, the practice borders on illegal in certain areas. So before using one of these apps, you are supposed to look up the laws in your country.

Smartphones allow us to record our conversations now, but not all Android devices provide this functionality. Some of these may work in Android 8.0 and earlier and some may work in Android 9 and 10. Thus, we could not guarantee anything here. We got all of these to work at least once on at least one tester phone and on at least one version of Android.

We are here to help you by providing the best call recording apps to keep a copy of the phone conversations (with the use of phone recorders) becomes easy for you.

1. Automatic Call Recorder

Price: Free trial / $6.99 per month

Automatic Call Recorder is one of the most renowned call recorder apps for Android. Just follow the usual method of giving the app audio and call access, and you will be able to record phone conversations. Calls will be added to the app in different sections, namely incoming calls, outgoing calls, all calls, or important calls.

The app features Google Drive and Dropbox integration along with some other neat little functions. The pro version is expensive but adds a few additional features as well. It works well aside from the call log thing.

2. Blackbox Call Recorder

Price: Free trial / $0.99 per month

Blackbox is known as a professional call recorder app. Its features include call recording, cloud backup support, and recording quality settings. However, this one takes it a step further with a lock function for security, Bluetooth accessory support, and dual SIM support.

The app has one-sided recording on some carriers, non-compatibility with other voice recording apps, power-saving mode, and permissions it takes before commencing the process.

Once opened, the app displays all the call recordings and has a hamburger menu on the left side and a search logo at the right to look for specific call recordings quickly.

If you win an important award in an industry, put it in your email signature or as a tagline in a piece of social content. Showcasing your wins organically and authentically will attract more of the same.

To sum up, the call recordings can be sorted as per duration, date, name, and more, making things convenient.
